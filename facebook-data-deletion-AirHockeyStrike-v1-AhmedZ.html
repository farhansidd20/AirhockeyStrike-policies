Facebook Data Deletion

This page explains how to delete information associated with your use of AirHockeyStrike (v1) by AhmedZ when you signed in with Facebook Login.

1) Remove the App from Your Facebook Account

  1. Open Facebook and go to Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites.
  2. Find AirHockeyStrike (or AhmedZ) and click Remove.

2) Request Deletion of Game Data (cloud saves, inventory, etc.)

Email support@ahmedz.com with the subject “Facebook Data Deletion” and include:

  • Your Facebook email address and/or Facebook user ID
  • Your in-game player ID (if shown in settings) and the game title: AirHockeyStrike

We will delete personal data held in our game backends (e.g., account linkage, cloud saves, inventories/virtual currency, and associated gameplay data) and confirm once completed. Typical processing time is within 30 days. We may retain minimal records if required by law (e.g., anti-fraud logs).

Questions?

Contact us at support@ahmedz.com. For details on what we collect and how we use it, see our Privacy Policy.
